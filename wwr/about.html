Our Story

River Rush Adventures was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate river guides dedicated to safe, sustainable, and unforgettable whitewater experiences.

Our Mission

Deliver outstanding river adventures while protecting waterways and educating guests on stewardship and safety.

  • Certified guides and modern safety equipment
  • Low-impact rafting and leave-no-trace camping
  • Community partnerships and conservation efforts

Our History

Starting as a small local guide service, we've expanded into a trusted regional outfitter. We still keep the family-feel and personal attention to each group.
